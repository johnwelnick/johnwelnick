who i am

John Welnick

Somewhere between the study and the prayer meeting, I found that the two rooms belong to the same house. The doctrines of grace and the gifts of the Spirit are not rivals — they are old friends.

I write and teach for believers who love the confessions and the concordance, but who also believe God still speaks, heals, and moves. Call it Edwards with the windows open.

Sola Scriptura. Sola gratia. And yes — earnestly desire the spiritual gifts.

the convictions

Three Pillars of the House

Everything taught here rests on the same foundation: the Word of God, rightly divided and powerfully applied.

I.

Sovereign Grace

God saves sinners — beginning to end. We stand with the old paths: the doctrines of grace, the great confessions, and a big, unembarrassed view of God's sovereignty.

II.

The Spirit's Power

The same Spirit who inspired the Book still fills the church. Prophecy, healing, and prayer in power are not relics — they are the ordinary equipment of an extraordinary gospel.

III.

The Ordinary Church

Word and sacrament, prayer and fellowship. Revival isn't an escape from the local church — it's what happens when God visits one.
